Featured Cards

ODIN

Odin, the Allfather, ruler of Asgard and god of wisdom, war, and poetry. With a single eye, he gazes deep into the well of knowledge, guided by his trusted ravens, Huginn and Muninn. As the chief deity, he seeks knowledge, power, and the understanding of life's mysteries.

THOR

Thor, the mighty god of thunder, wields Mjölnir, a hammer of unparalleled power. Protector of both gods and humans, his strength and valor are legendary. With each thunderclap, enemies tremble, for the storm heralds the approach of the Thunderer.

FREYJA

Freyja, the goddess of love, beauty, and fertility, reigns over the radiant realm of Fólkvangr. Riding her chariot drawn by cats, she's a fierce warrior and sorceress. In her hall, she welcomes half of those who die in battle, proving love and war are two sides of the same coin.

HEIMDALL

Heimdall, the vigilant sentinel of the gods, stands guard at the Bifrost bridge, ever watchful. With keen senses that can hear grass grow and see the end of the universe, he readies his Gjallarhorn, prepared to signal the onset of Ragnarök.

Seek not just wisdom in books and tales, but in the silent whispers of the wind and the patterns of the stars. For true knowledge lies in understanding the language of the universe.

- Thor, God of Thunder
